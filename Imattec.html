Products

Technical Fibers & Yarns
For innovative textile solutions

Immatec International develops and supplies quality engineering textile materials using high performance fibres to satisfy even the most sophisticated and demanding clientele

Stainless steel

Stainless steel (inox) fibers have high electrical conductivity, high temperature withstanding, very poor elongation, resistance to chemical corrosion. High density.

Aramids

Aramids fibers have high temperature withstanding, high mechanical properties, no melting point, good fatigue resistance, very good chemical resistance.

IMAT'Fire®

IMAT'Fire is a non-flamable fibre used in fire and heat conditions as well in protective clothes as in specific industrial applications. It shows great insulating performance and an outstanding behahiour while it is exposed to fire : No burning, no melting, no dripping, no smoking, no shrinking.

Vectran

Vectran® is a high-performance fibre spun from liquid crystal polymer made by Kuraray. This fiber has exceptional strength and rigidity : 5 times stronger than steel, 10 times stronger than aluminum, excellent performances in abrasion, cut protection and exceptional resistance to high temperatures variations: from -80°C up to 350°C.IMATTEC Int. has worked in close partnership with KURARAY USA to develop the long fibre spun yarn : pure Vectran or blended with other fibres.

PTFE

Lenzing Profilen is 100% PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) fibre. This fibre is where high temperature resistance and stability against chemicals is required. Temperature resistant from -212ºC to 288ºC. Chemically inert to acids, caustics, solvents and hydrocarbons (0-14pH). Low elongation Non adhesive / low coefficient of friction. Non inflammable. Hydrophobic (no moisture regain, no hydrolysis).
